About the Campaign

The Light of Hope campaign is transforming traditional donations. By microfinancing clean water wells, every contributor becomes part of a revolutionary system. Donate small amounts and see your name on a community well plaque, showcasing collective impact.

Our Goals

  • Raise $50,000 by pooling micro-donations.
  • Build sustainable clean water wells with community involvement.
  • Engage younger audiences through modern platforms and shared ownership.

Implementation Strategy

We plan to engage the community and amplify impact through:

  • Influencer-Led Marketing: Collaborate with global figures like Mr. Beast to drive awareness and inspire action.
  • Documentaries and Videos: Share stories of those in need, showcasing the transformative power of clean water.
  • Microfinancing Platform: Simplify contributions and highlight donor recognition with plaques.

Timeline

From January to June 2024, our campaign will focus on building wells through community microfinancing, with heightened engagement during Ramadan and continuous video updates from impacted areas.
